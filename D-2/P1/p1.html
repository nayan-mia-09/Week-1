Tribute to A.P.J Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

A.P.J Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist, know for his work in India's space and missile programs.

Achievemnts

  • Developed India's first satelite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
